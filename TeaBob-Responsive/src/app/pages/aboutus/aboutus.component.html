ABOUT US

Teabob is a small food business residing in the Municipality of Floridablanca, Pampanga. The business started on the month of July, 2020. The business owner, Mrs. Loreta Balatbat, decided to start the business in the said year to earn revenue in the midst of the pandemic. Teabob offers an array of food items, ranging from refreshing milk teas, to delicious hearty foods like shawarma and nachos. Since then, the business has been well-known to serve delicious food to their customers. And now it is even better, because Teabob has now come online and is ready to take your order!
